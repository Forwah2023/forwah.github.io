PdfResearch

A fast text-folder search desktop app supporting multiple keywords across PDF, DOC, and TXT files.

About

PdfResearch is a multi-threaded desktop application that performs fast, multi-keyword searches for text patterns across folders containing .pdf, .doc, and .txt files. The inspiration for this app resulted from a shortage of free software capable of performing multi-keyword searches on desktop folders. Traditional desktop applications in this category only allow users to search for one keyword at a time. Furthermore, they were not as fast as I needed them to be. This app leverages some of the fastest text-extracting Python-based packages available online to build a free app that competes with paid online versions.
